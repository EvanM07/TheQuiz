About the ASVAB General Science Quiz

The Armed Services Vocational Aptitude Battery (ASVAB) General Science test assesses your knowledge of scientific concepts across a range of disciplines, including biology, chemistry, physics, and earth science. This quiz is designed to help you practice and prepare for the General Science section of the ASVAB.

The quiz consists of 30 multiple-choice questions.

Good luck, and have fun!
